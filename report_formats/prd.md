# PRD Format

Use this format for Codex-authored PM Assistant PRDs. Base claims on internal evidence and cited research only.

Required sections:

```markdown
# <Topic> PRD

## Problem

## Evidence

## Goals

## Non-goals

## Requirements

## Success Metrics

## Open Questions
```

Rules:
- Cite customer evidence with original Webex, Aha, or Jira links when available.
- Keep external desk research in a clearly labeled paragraph or bullet with URLs.
- Use "Not available" instead of inventing metrics, opportunity value, owners, or dates.
- Keep requirements testable and avoid committing engineering design details unless the evidence already supports them.
