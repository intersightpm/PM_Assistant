# Research Brief Format

Use this format for cited external desk research.

Required sections:

```markdown
# <Topic> Research Brief

## Key Findings

## Sources

## Implications For Product

## Risks And Unknowns
```

Rules:
- Every factual market, competitor, standard, or product claim needs a URL citation.
- Include date accessed for each source when known.
- Do not mix external research with internal customer evidence unless explicitly comparing them.
